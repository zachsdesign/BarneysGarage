About Us

Since opening our doors in 2023, Barney’s Auto has been committed to delivering quality auto repair services you can trust. Our service, parts, and management teams bring decades of hands-on experience from their tenure at Massy Motors Limited, giving your vehicle the benefit of unmatched skill and expertise.

What We Specialize In

  • Engine and transmission replacement or repair
  • Fuel and ignition systems diagnostics and service
  • Cooling and lubrication systems maintenance
  • Electrical system repairs
  • Suspension and steering adjustments
  • Brake system repairs
  • Exhaust system maintenance

Our skilled technicians use state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and high-quality parts to ensure your car receives the best care possible. Whether it’s a routine oil change or a major repair, we tailor our services to meet your vehicle’s specific needs.

At Barney’s Auto, preventative maintenance is at the heart of what we do. We believe in honest assessments, transparent pricing, and clear communication—so you always know what to expect before any work begins.

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We aim to build long-lasting relationships with every customer by delivering exceptional service that keeps you safe and confident on the road.

Our Vision

To provide seamless, reliable auto repair that gives you peace of mind during every drive.

And in those unexpected moments, our in-house roadside assistance is just a phone call away—ready to get you back on the road quickly and safely.
